Our Story

Who We Are

A New Discipline.
A Singular Focus.

"We believe that the most memorable spaces are not just seen — they are felt, deeply and subconsciously."

LA Scenting was founded on a singular conviction: that every great space deserves a scent identity as considered as its architecture. We are the official scent curators and designers for Changi Airport Singapore — the world's award-winning international gateway — and a trusted partner to some of the world's most discerning brands. We work at the intersection of olfactory science, spatial design, and luxury brand strategy.

The Founder

Aaliyah Hamdoon

"We create a silent logo — an olfactory identity that speaks without words and forms instant, lasting connections."

With 18 years of industry experience, Aaliyah brings a nuanced understanding of how scent shapes memory, behaviour and perception. Together with her team, she designs environments and experiential events that stir the body, soul and mind into meaningful action.

Her portfolio includes serving as the official scent curator and designer for Changi Airport Singapore. From corporate to retail, functional to artistic, LA Scenting encodes a distinct perception of every brand — unlocking an atmosphere that elevates each experience.
